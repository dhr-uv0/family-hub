📋 What is Family Hub?

Family Hub is a shared app for your whole family — think of it as a digital family command center. Everyone in the family can open the same app, see the same information in real-time, and add or update content from any device.

Step 1 — Create Your Family Accounts

⚠️ One-time setup — do this first before anyone installs the app Each family member needs their own account. The first person to sign up should be a parent (they become the Admin).
  • 1
    Open a web browser and go to the app On any device: type family-hub-seven.vercel.app in the address bar and press Enter
  • 2
    Tap "Sign up" to create the first account (Parent 1 — Admin) Enter your name, email address, and a password. This account will be the family Admin.
  • 3
    Check your email and confirm your account You'll receive a confirmation email. Click the link inside it. Then come back and log in.
  • 4
    Repeat for each family member Each person does the same on their own device — or you can sign them up from one device first, then they log in on their own. Use a different email per person.
  • 5
    Admin: Go to Settings → Family Members → Add each person After signing in, tap Settings in the sidebar. Add each family member's name, pick a colour, and assign their role (Admin for parents, Member for kids).
💡 Tip — Shared family email alternative: If your kids don't have their own email, you can use Gmail aliases. For example, if your email is yourname@gmail.com, use yourname+kid1@gmail.com — Gmail delivers it to the same inbox.

Step 2 — Install the App on Every Device

Family Hub works in any browser, but installing it as an app gives you a home screen icon, full-screen experience, and faster loading. Here's how to do it on each platform:

📱 iPhone & iPad (iOS)

Using Safari (required for iOS)

  1. Open Safari (must be Safari, not Chrome)
  2. Go to family-hub-seven.vercel.app
  3. Tap the Share button at the bottom (box with arrow pointing up ↑)
  4. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
  5. Change the name to Family Hub if needed
  6. Tap Add in the top right

✅ The app now appears on your home screen like any other app. Tap it to open full-screen.

💻 Windows PC & Laptop

Using Chrome or Edge (recommended)

  1. Open Chrome or Microsoft Edge
  2. Go to family-hub-seven.vercel.app
  3. Look for the install icon in the address bar (a circle with a + or a computer icon)
  4. Click it and select "Install"
  5. The app opens in its own window, no browser tabs needed

✅ Find it in your Start Menu or Desktop as "Family Hub". Works like a native app.

📌 No installation? No problem. You don't have to install it. Every family member can also just open a browser and go to family-hub-seven.vercel.app — it works exactly the same. Installation just makes it faster to open.

Step 3 — Signing In

  • 1
    Open the app Either tap the home screen icon (installed) or go to family-hub-seven.vercel.app in a browser
  • 2
    Enter your email and password, then tap "Sign in" You'll be taken straight to the Dashboard.
  • 3
    Prefer not to type a password? Use Magic Link Tap "Send magic link" on the login screen, enter your email, and click the link in the email you receive. No password needed.
  • 4
    Stay signed in Your session lasts 7 days. On a family device (like a kitchen tablet), you can stay permanently signed in and never have to log in again.

Step 4 — Getting Around the App

On Phone (iPhone)

A bottom tab bar appears at the bottom of the screen with the main sections. Tap "More" to see all pages.

On iPad & Windows (tablet/desktop)

A sidebar appears on the left with all pages listed. Click any item to navigate.

Family Mode vs Personal Mode

At the top of every page there is a toggle switch:

Mode What you see Who can see it
🏠 Family Default Shared calendar, shopping, meals, chores, announcements, photos Everyone in the family
👤 Personal Your private tasks, personal calendar, private to-do lists Only you

Step 5 — Using Each Feature

🏠 Dashboard

The home screen everyone sees when they open the app. Shows:

  • Live clock — current time and date
  • Weather — current conditions for your city (set city in Settings)
  • Today's Schedule — all family events happening today
  • Who Is Home — each family member's status (Home/Away/School/Work/Running Late). Tap your own card to update your status.
  • Announcements — important family messages at the top
  • Photo of the Day — a random family photo
  • Morning Briefing — appears 6–10am with a summary of the day

📅 Family Calendar

  • Switch between Month, Week, and Day views
  • Each family member's events appear in their colour
  • Tap any day to add an event, or tap an existing event to edit it
  • Set recurring events (e.g. weekly soccer practice)
  • All changes appear on everyone's devices instantly

🛒 Shopping Lists

  • You can have multiple lists (e.g. Grocery, Hardware, Costco)
  • Tap + to add an item to the active list
  • Tap the checkbox next to an item to mark it done
  • Tap "Clear done" to remove all completed items
  • If someone adds an item on their phone, it appears on your screen in seconds — no refresh needed
  • Items are grouped by category (Produce, Dairy, etc.)
💡 Shopping list tip: When you're at the store, open the app on your phone. As you pick up items, tap the checkbox. Your partner at home will see the list update in real-time and can add forgotten items while you're still shopping.

✅ Tasks (Personal Mode)

  • Switch to Personal Mode to see your private task list
  • Add tasks with: title, due date, priority (Low / Medium / High / Urgent), category, and tags
  • Overdue tasks turn red automatically
  • Today's tasks are highlighted in amber
  • Only you can see your personal tasks — not other family members
  • Turn on Family Task to make a task visible to everyone

📆 My Calendar (Personal Smart Calendar)

Three views available via tabs at the top:

  • Today — your agenda for today: time-blocked events + priority-ranked tasks
  • List — all your tasks grouped by date (Overdue, Today, Tomorrow, This Week, Later)
  • Calendar — full monthly/weekly calendar showing both your events (blocks) and tasks (dots on due date)

🍽️ Meal Planner

  • 7-day grid with Breakfast, Lunch, and Dinner rows
  • Tap any empty slot to add a meal (title, description, who's cooking, prep time)
  • Add a recipe URL to link to the recipe website
  • Tap "Add to Shopping List" on any meal to add ingredients

✨ Chore Chart

  • Weekly grid: rows = chores, columns = Mon–Sun
  • Tap a cell to mark a chore as done (green checkmark)
  • Points are awarded per completion
  • Leaderboard at the top shows who has the most points this week — great for kids!

📸 Photos

  • Auto-rotating carousel at the top (changes every 30 seconds)
  • All photos displayed in a grid below
  • Tap the Upload button to add a photo from your device
  • Add a caption and it appears for everyone

📣 Announcements

  • Post important messages for the whole family
  • Set a priority (Low / Medium / High / Urgent) — urgent ones appear in red
  • Set an expiry date so old announcements disappear automatically
  • Pin important announcements so they stay at the top
  • React with emoji 👍❤️😂😮

ℹ️ Important Info

A permanent reference hub for things the whole family needs. Organised into categories:

  • 🚨 Emergency Contacts
  • 📶 WiFi Passwords
  • 🏫 School Portals & Links
  • 🔧 Service Providers
  • 📋 Other

Tap Copy on any entry to copy the content to your clipboard instantly.

🎤 Voice Input

Tap the microphone button (bottom-right corner of the screen) and speak a command:

  • "Add milk to shopping list"
  • "Add task — call the dentist"
  • "Show calendar"
  • "Go to shopping"
📱 Voice requires microphone permission: The first time you use it, your device will ask permission to use the microphone. Tap "Allow". Voice works in Safari (iOS) and Chrome/Edge (Windows).

How Real-Time Sync Works

Family Hub uses live database connections. When any family member makes a change:

  • 1
    You add "Milk" to the shopping list on your iPhone The item is saved to the shared database instantly
  • 2
    Everyone else's app receives an automatic notification No manual refresh needed — the update appears on their screen within 1–2 seconds
  • 3
    All devices always show the same data Whether on the kitchen iPad, your iPhone, or a Windows laptop at work
✅ Works across all devices simultaneously You can have the shopping list open on the kitchen tablet AND on your phone at the store — both will update in real-time as either person makes changes.

Updating Your Location Status

On the Dashboard, the "Who Is Home" widget shows where everyone is. Each person updates their own status:

  1. Go to the Dashboard
  2. Find the "Who Is Home" section
  3. Tap your own card (it says "tap to update")
  4. Choose your status: 🏠 Home · 🚗 Away · ⏰ Running Late · 📚 School · 💼 Work

Everyone else sees your status update immediately.

Admin Settings (Parents Only)

The family Admin (first account created) can manage family settings:

Managing Family Members

  1. Go to Settings in the sidebar
  2. Scroll to Family Members
  3. Tap Add Member to add a new family member
  4. Set their name, nickname, colour (used for their calendar events), role, and date of birth
  5. Use the toggle to activate or deactivate a member

Weather City

  1. Go to Settings → App Settings
  2. Type your city name in the Weather City box (e.g. "Chicago, US")
  3. Tap Save — the weather widget on the Dashboard will now show your city

Troubleshooting

The app isn't updating in real-time

Check your internet connection. Real-time sync requires an active internet connection. Pull down to refresh if needed.

I can't install it on my iPhone — I don't see "Add to Home Screen"

Make sure you're using Safari (not Chrome or Firefox on iOS). The Share button must be tapped from Safari for the option to appear.

I forgot my password

On the login page, tap "Send magic link" and enter your email. You'll get an email link to sign in without a password.

The app shows a setup banner

This means the database isn't connected yet. Contact the family Admin — they need to complete the Supabase setup.

I can hear the microphone click but voice input isn't working

On iOS, go to Settings → Safari → Microphone and make sure it's allowed. On Windows, check browser permissions in the address bar (🔒 icon).
